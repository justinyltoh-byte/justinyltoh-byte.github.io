About Me

I am a postdoctoral scientist with a Ph.D. in Microbial Pathogenesis from Yale University. My research spans protein engineering, RNA biology, and parasite biology — from directed evolution of antibody mimetics to transcriptomic analysis of infectious parasites.

I am passionate about using computational and molecular tools to answer fundamental biological questions. This site documents my ongoing reanalysis of published datasets and the scripts I use to generate figures.
